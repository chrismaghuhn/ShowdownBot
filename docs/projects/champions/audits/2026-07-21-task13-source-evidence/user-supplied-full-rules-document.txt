Tournament Format & Rules – Pokémon Champions VGC
This tournament will be conducted using a Swiss-style format in Pokémon Champions, followed by a Top Cut based on standings.
Event Details
* Location: Busch Student Center – Rutgers University
* Address: 604 Bartholomew Rd, Piscataway, NJ 08854
* Date: Saturday, May 2nd
* Check-In: 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM
* Tournament Start Time: 1:15 PM
Players are strongly encouraged to arrive early. Late arrivals may not be permitted to participate once the tournament has begun.
Entry Fee
* Rutgers Students: $10 (must present a valid Rutgers ID / RUID)
* Non-Rutgers Participants: $15
Prize Pool
* This event will feature a $1,000 starting prize pool, consisting of a combination of cash and Pokémon Perfect Order TCG products, distributed as follows:
   * 1st Place: $300 + Booster Box
   * 2nd Place: $150 + Booster Box
   * 3rd–4th Place: $75 + Elite Trainer Box (ETB)
   * 5th–8th Place: $30 + Booster Bundle
* The total prize pool may increase based on the number of participants and total sign-ups.
* Winners may be required to complete a tax form in accordance with Rutgers University prizing policies prior to receiving their prizes.
Game Format
* The tournament will follow the official VGC Doubles format.
* Matches will be played as Best-of-Three (Bo3) sets.
* Players will register a team of six (6) Pokémon.
* For each game within a set, players will select four (4) Pokémon from their registered team to battle.
Ruleset
* The format will follow the Standard Regulation M–A ruleset, including the use of Mega Evolution, limited to Pokémon currently available in Pokémon Champions.
* All Pokémon, moves, abilities, and items must comply with the legal standards defined by the game and tournament format.
Team Sheet Policy
This is an Open Team Sheet tournament, with the following requirements:
* Players must submit:
   1. A Closed Team Sheet, including:
      * Exact Pokémon
      * Moves
      * Abilities
      * Held items
      * Stat Spreads
      * Levels
   2. An Open Team Sheet, including:
      * Pokémon
      * Moves
      * Abilities
      * Held items
* Open Team Sheets will be made available to opponents before each match.
Wi-Fi Requirement & MAC Address Submission
* Due to network restrictions, players must submit their Nintendo Switch MAC address in advance to gain access to the event Wi-Fi.
* Failure to submit a MAC address may result in lack of Wi-Fi access, and tournament organizers are not responsible for providing connectivity in such cases.
How to Find Your Nintendo Switch MAC Address
1. From the HOME Menu, select System Settings
2. Scroll down and select Internet
3. Select Internet Settings
4. Your MAC Address will be displayed on this screen
Additional Notes
* Players are expected to adhere to all tournament rules and maintain good sportsmanship.
* Any disputes or rule clarifications will be handled at the discretion of the tournament organizers.
