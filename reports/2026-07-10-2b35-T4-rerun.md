## 8. Erratum — T4 FAIL report §5 (root cause, RC-2)

The T4 FAIL report described the unseeded endpoint as reached at `decision.py:632`
(`choose_with_fallback` → `pick_random_pair`). Correction: the unseeded endpoint was reached via
`max_damage_choice`'s **INTERNAL default fallback** (`battle/baselines.py:43-44`, pre-fix), **one
level earlier** than the report's `decision.py:632` description. The **observable endpoint** — an
unseeded `pick_random_pair` producing a nondeterministic replacement slot/order — is **unchanged**;
only the call path that reached it is corrected here.
